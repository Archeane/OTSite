Theta Tau


Engineering Leaders for Service, Profession, and Brotherhood

Theta Tau Zeta Epsilon Chapter at Stony Brook University

At over 35,000 members, Theta Tau is the oldest, largest, and foremost Fraternity for Engineers.

At Stony Brook University, we are the only coed professional engineering fraternity on campus. We focus on developing and maintaining a high standard of professional interest among our members, and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship. Our members are: Honest and Ethical, Dependable and Trustworthy, Respectful, Generous, and Proud of our Community of Engineers.

As a professional engineering fraternity, we combine aspects found in that of social fraternities and technical-oriented societies to create a community where brothers can form life-long bonds, develop themselves professionally, and have meaningful social experiences.

For more information, visit our national website.
